Important Information

Footware:

There is a short walk along a pebble beach from the boat to Laganini, we advise bringing a flat pair of shoes and changing into shoes upon arrival. Laganini is a tiered venue with steps, we do advise block heels!

Confetti:

Laganini is in a beautiful natural area, please note that paper confetti is not allowed. Please feel free to use natural petals instead.

Area and Accomodation

Ciovo Island

Our recommendation is to stay in Trogir and Čiovo Island, these are are the closest options to Laganini. The boat service will have pick up points from both Trogir and Slatine - on Ciovo Island.

The island of Čiovo, is connected to the mainland via a bridge. Offering beautiful beaches, serene nature, and a relaxing escape. Okrug Gornji is the main village with beaches, restaurants/cafes on the beach, water-sports and shops.

There are other smaller villages on Ciovo, all with beautiful beaches and amenities. Most of the beaches in Croatia are small pebble beaches, this is what gives the water such an incredible colour!

Trogir

Trogir town is a charming historic town known for its well-preserved medieval architecture and UNESCO World Heritage status. There are plenty of shops and restaurants and the beaches of Ciovo are easily accessible.

Want to see more while you're here? Trogir marina provides easy boat connections to Split.

Brown Beach House

We recommend Brown Beach House in Trogir. The old town of Trogir is a 15 min walk from the hotel with shops, restaurants and nightlife. There is a small private beach as part of the hotel and there are plenty of beaches within a 10-15 min drive.

The hotel is a 15 minute drive from the airport and 600m from the Trogir boat pick up point on the day of the wedding. It's also 15 minutes drive to Laganini should you choose to make your own way there.

Bookings for June 2026 will be available from October 2025.
