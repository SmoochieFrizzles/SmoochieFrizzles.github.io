Event Timeline

This will be a timeline of events for the big day

  • Split boat service

    1:00pm

    To start the event timeline, A boat service from Split to Trogir will depart for anyone staying in Split

  • Trogir boat service

    1:30pm

    Then once the boat arrives at Trogir to pick up anyone staying in Trogir, the same boat will then depart for Slatine

  • To the venue

    2:00pm

    The same boat will then transport the guests from Slatine to the Venue, you will need to arrive on time otherwise you will need to find alternative travel, you can get a taxi.

  • At the Venue

    2:30pm

    There will be a short walk from the dock to the venue (3 mins), You will be greated with a glass of bubbly, now is the time to relax and socialize until the ceremony.

  • The Ceremony

    3:00pm

    The ceremony will be held overlooking the beautiful beach and the clear bay, it will be a short ceremony but it is standing, if you have any issues with this, please let us know beforehand.

  • Appetisers, Cocktails & Pictures

    3:30pm

    Once the ceremony has been completed, it is now the time for appetisers and socializing, the photographer will also be taking photos, we will need to coordinate some group pictures

  • Toast & Speeches

    4:30pm

    After the group photos the time will come for speeches and toasts, no bad language

  • Dinner

    5:00pm

    Dinner time, the dinner service will be a buffet, feel free to eat when you like

Shoe Information

The walk to the venue

From the dock to the venue
There will be steps and part gravel road

With traveling on a boat and then arriving at the venue the boat will dock on the far side of the peninsula, there will be a short walk (3 min) to the venue from the dock. this walk will include steps and a small gravel path.

Due to this we advise that you bring flat pair of shoes, at least to get to the venue, you can change into heels once you have arrived

Please note the venue does have steps, it is a tiered structure
